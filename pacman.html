Pacman

Released in 1980 by Namco

Pacman is possibly the most iconic arcade cabinet, even today. The gameplay is simple: steer Pacman, the titular yellow wide-mouthed character across the board, collecting all the dots and avoiding the ghosts. This simple gameplay, along with the overall appealing and eye-catching design of both the game and cabinet, skyrocketed it to international fame.

It is estimated that the Pacman franchise has generated over $14 billion dollars in revenue across over 30 total games, including the ever popular Ms. Pacman, featuring new sprites but classic gameplay.
